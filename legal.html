Purpose & Scope

Appendix Batcher is a client-side utility used to compile screenshots or PDFs into paginated appendices and overlay user-entered identification text. It does not create, modify, store, or transmit regulated electronic records, and it does not implement electronic signatures. Output PDFs are presentation copies that customers include in their controlled documentation set.

Regulatory Position

  • Operates entirely within the user’s browser; no file uploads or data retention.
  • Not a system of record under 21 CFR Part 11 or EU Annex 11.
  • User-typed text provides attribution only and is not an electronic signature.
  • Final signed or controlled copies reside in the customer’s repository (SharePoint/QMS/binder).

ALCOA+ / GDP Alignment

  • Attributable – Operator manually enters identifying text.
  • Legible – Header placed in non-obscuring margin.
  • Contemporaneous – Annotation applied at compilation time; final signature per customer SOP.
  • Original / Accurate – Source files unaltered; outputs are faithful copies.
  • Complete / Enduring – Full appendix sets; durable PDFs; retained under customer control.

Proper Use

  1. Collect required screenshots/PDFs per protocol.
  2. Type header text manually (e.g., initials, date, protocol ID).
  3. Generate PDFs (~50 pages each; do not split appendices).
  4. Ensure overlays do not obscure evidential data.
  5. Transfer outputs to the customer’s controlled repository and print/sign as required.
  6. Retain the original input files unchanged until customer acceptance.

Responsibilities

  • Operator: Accurate data collection, header entry, printing/signing.
  • Customer (Record Owner): Repository access, versioning, retention, and approval workflows.

Validation & Fitness-for-Purpose

Category: Non-GxP utility / document-preparation aid. Fitness checks are sufficient: header on each page, no obscuration, correct page size/margins, proper sorting/chunking, and unaltered source files. Full Part 11 validation is not required because the tool does not store records or implement signatures.

Security & Privacy

  • Processing occurs locally in the browser; nothing uploaded.
  • Do not enter personal or confidential data beyond necessary identifiers.
  • Handle files per customer confidentiality and retention policies.

Disclaimer

This utility assists in preparing and labeling validation appendices. User-entered text is overlaid for identification/traceability only. No source data are modified, stored, or electronically signed. Final printed or controlled copies remain the official records in accordance with customer procedures and GDP/ALCOA+ principles.
